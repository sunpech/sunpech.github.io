What was the problem? Computer virii, trojan horses, adware, and spyware made the system unstable. How did it get this way? Overall, it's because of not having a good understanding of computers, but in particular, peer-to-peer(P2P) programs such as BearShare.

Here is a list of the most common FREE programs I recommend for typical users:
  • Antivirus: Avast Home Edition or AVG Free
  • Firewall: Comodo Firewall
  • Web Browser: Mozilla Firefox
  • Spyware detection: Microsoft Defender
  • Image Editing: Paint.net
  • Compression: 7 Zip
  • Chat: Google Talk
  • Picture manager: Google Picasa
Other notable programs are:
  • Bittorrent client: utorrent (also get Peer Guardian)
  • Media Players: Winamp, VLC, and Divx
  • Office Suite: Open Office
  • Email client: Mozilla Thunderbird
  • FTP: Filezilla
If you feel this list is incomplete, check out: The 46 Best-ever Freeware Utilities
