I got a little tour of the place and was very impressed. I've been to a few coworking offices in LA already (CoLoft, BlankSpaces, and Kleverdog), and I can honestly say that this is my favorite to cowork in. One, it's conveniently located in Culver City, where I currently live in. Two, it's got a great amount of space, offices, and little nooks to work in. And third, there are a ton of people that cowork here. It just feels like a great place to be at, very comfortable.

NextSpace LA
9415 Culver Boulevard
Culver City, CA 90232
